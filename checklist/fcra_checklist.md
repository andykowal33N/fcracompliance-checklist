# FCRA Checklist (Ops-Facing)

## 1. Permissible Purpose
- [ ] Identify and document permissible purposes under 15 USC ยง1681b.
- [ ] Restrict access to approved personnel or functions.
- [ ] Audit logs confirm appropriate data usage.

## 2. Disclosure & Authorization
- [ ] Provide standalone disclosure (no mixed content).
- [ ] Obtain written/electronic authorization before processing.
- [ ] Retain signed acknowledgements.

## 3. Adverse Action
- [ ] Pre-adverse notice includes report copy + rights summary.
- [ ] Wait 5 business days before final action.
- [ ] Document communication and resolution.

## 4. Dispute & Reinvestigation
- [ ] Implement process for dispute submission and tracking.
- [ ] Complete reinvestigation within 30 days.
- [ ] Notify consumer of results and corrective actions.

## 5. Data Handling
- [ ] Role-based access controls applied.
- [ ] Secure destruction schedule established.
- [ ] Vendor APIs and exports logged.

## 6. Vendors & Audits
- [ ] Maintain agreements with CRAs and sub-processors.
- [ ] Conduct annual compliance audits.
- [ ] Capture attestations of FCRA adherence.
