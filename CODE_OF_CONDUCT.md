# Code of Conduct

We are building an open, respectful, and professional community around these projects.  
By participating, you agree to uphold these principles:

## 1. Be Respectful
Treat others with courtesy and professionalism.  
No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.

## 2. Be Constructive
Provide feedback that is specific, actionable, and meant to improve the project.  
Disagree without being disagreeable.

## 3. Stay Professional
Keep discussions and contributions focused on ideas, not individuals.  
Avoid political or unrelated topics.

## 4. Report Issues
If you encounter behavior that violates this code, open an Issue or contact the maintainers privately.

## 5. Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, comments, or contributors that violate these guidelines.
